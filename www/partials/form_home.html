With the FormBuilder, you can design your own forms from scratch by following 3 simple steps:

  1. Click the "New Form” button.
  2. Drag & drop form components from the right site of the screen into the left side.
  3. Press the “Save” button whenever you want to save your progress.

You can come back to this page by clicking the “Home” button in the top bar. It will then show you a list of all your forms.
